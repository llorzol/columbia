Sections: A graphic section along the line segment(s) between locations will appear. The section is drawn from left to right in the order locations were selected.

  • To zoom, select a rectangle in the smaller overview graphic by using the left button of the mouse to slowly drag an area.
  • To reset, place the cursor over the smaller overview graphic and double click the left button of the mouse.
  • To reactivate the red tracker line, place the cursor over the larger graphic and single click the left button of the mouse.
